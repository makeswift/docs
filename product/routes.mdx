<Note>Routes that are not Makeswift pages cannot be edited in the page builder.</Note>

Routes organize your pages by [pathname](/product/page/paths), providing a sitemap-like view that makes it easier to find and navigate between pages across your entire site.

<Frame>![ Makeswift builder Pages Tab with Routes](/images/routes.png)</Frame>

## Enabling routes

To enable routes on your site, you must turn on the feature in the Early Access dialog window.

- In the bottom of the Site Sidebar, click **Settings**.
- In the Settings dialog window, select the **Early Access** option.
- Enable the **Path-based Pages Sidebar** early access feature.
